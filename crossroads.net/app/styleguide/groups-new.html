Group FAQs
Where do Groups meet? Groups meet in homes, restaurants, and even the local coffee shop. Some Groups meet to run together and others may go for a hike. We can help you find a Group near you based on your passions and interests. How often do Groups meet? Groups meet throughout the week at different days and times. What can I expect from my Group? It depends on the Group you select. Some serve on the same team at church while other Groups study the Bible together.
