Group FAQs

What is a Group?

They come in all shapes and sizes, but all share a few common bonds. They all have a leader who follows Jesus, meet regularly and focus on exploring God and building relationships.

Where do they meet?

They’re designed to be integrated into your life vs. adding to your already busy schedule. So if you spend a lot of time at the gym, coffee shop, office or with your kids, search for groups with similar interests.

What should I expect?

It depends on the type you choose. Some are just exploring God together through a shared common interest or neighborhood, and some dive deeper into discipleship and mission.

What about kids?

Each group figures out a plan that works for them. Many groups share the cost of a sitter or rotate houses. Look for the “kid friendly” option within the Group Tool, and find one that works for you.
